Create a New Access Identity

An Access Identity is basically an account that you have to make on this portal.

It allows you to have your own Access Folder which is securely encrypted for you to store data.


Each Access Identity is attached to a automatically generated login certificate.

This login certificate expires after 30 days which means that you will have to renew your certificate with a renewal code.
